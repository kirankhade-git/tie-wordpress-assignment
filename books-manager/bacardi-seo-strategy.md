# Bacardi SEO & Website Strategy

## Objective

Bacardi wants to improve its visibility for cocktail-related searches and attract users who are actively looking for drink recipes and home mixology ideas. At the same time, the website should reduce its bounce rate and improve user engagement.

---

## High Intent Keywords

The following keywords have strong commercial and informational intent and are highly relevant to Bacardi's products:

1. Bacardi mojito recipe
2. Easy rum cocktails at home
3. Bacardi cocktail recipes
4. Best white rum cocktail recipes
5. How to make rum punch

These keywords target users who are already interested in making cocktails and are more likely to engage with Bacardi content.

---

## On-Page SEO Recommendations

### Improve Content Structure

Recipe pages should follow a consistent structure:

- Introduction
- Ingredients
- Step-by-step instructions
- Preparation time
- Tips and variations
- Frequently Asked Questions

Using clear H1 and H2 headings will improve both readability and SEO.

### Improve Metadata

Each recipe page should have:

- Unique title tag
- Optimized meta description
- Descriptive image alt text
- Open Graph tags for social sharing

### Strengthen Internal Linking

Internal links should connect:

- Cocktail recipes
- Product pages
- Blog articles
- Related recipes

This helps users discover more content and improves crawlability.

---

## UX Audit

### Issue 1: Important information is difficult to find

Many users want ingredients and preparation steps immediately.

**Recommendation:** Place recipe details higher on the page and reduce unnecessary introductory content.

### Issue 2: Mobile experience may not be optimized

A large percentage of users search for recipes from mobile devices.

**Recommendation:** Improve mobile layouts, button sizes, spacing, and page speed.

### Issue 3: Lack of engagement opportunities

Users may leave after viewing a single recipe.

**Recommendation:** Add related recipes, cocktail suggestions, video tutorials, and clear calls to action.

---

## Local SEO Strategy

### 1. Create City-Specific Event Pages

Dedicated pages can be created for:

- Mumbai
- Pune
- Delhi
- Bangalore
- Hyderabad

Each page should contain event information, venue details, dates, maps, and FAQs.

### 2. Implement Event Schema Markup

Structured data will help Bacardi-sponsored events appear more prominently in search results.

### 3. Build Local Authority

Partner with:

- Local bars
- Event organizers
- Lifestyle blogs
- Food and beverage influencers

This can generate relevant backlinks and improve local search visibility.

---

## Expected Outcome

By improving content structure, strengthening internal linking, optimizing mobile usability, and implementing local SEO initiatives, Bacardi can improve search rankings, reduce bounce rate, increase engagement, and generate more organic traffic from cocktail-related searches.
